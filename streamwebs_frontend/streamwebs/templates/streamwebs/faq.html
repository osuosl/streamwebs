How do I access my old account?

You will receieve an email to the account you signed up with on the original site. From there you can reset your password and use the same account on the new site!



What's different about the site?

Not only has the site been given a modern face-lift, there are new features that we are excited aboutm such as:

  • You now have the ability to extract the data that uo have entered into the site, in the form of an excep spreadsheet for further manipulation in the classroom.

  • You can now compare sites and their information side-by-side! Be done with switching tabs to compare water qualities between two sites!

  • The water quality graphs have been simplified for a cleaner viewing experience, with less clutter and verbose data. Individual data points are averages of all the data entries submitted that day. You can now click on these to view as histograms.

  • Some of our less-used datasheets are no longer supported for entry into the new site. These include the Invasive Species, StreamFlow, and Pebble Count datasheets. These are still available in the resource section of the site in PDF format.

  • You will also notice that individual users no longer have a profile page, but you can still find all of your data located on the sites that you have participated in.
What if I run into an unexpected error?
As with any new website, there are still some bugs to work out (we like to call these unexpected features!). If you do find any bugs, send us an email at info@streamwebs.org and we will work to resolve them as quickly as we can.
